I'm a Frontend Developer & SEO Specialist who understands that a beautiful website means nothing if nobody finds it — and a highly-ranked page means nothing if users don't stay.

Based in Baluwatar, Kathmandu, I've built my career at the intersection of design, development, and search. My journey began in SEO — working with AU-based clients, running technical audits, developing keyword strategies on SEMrush, and measuring performance across Google's ecosystem.

Today I write interfaces that are fast, semantic, accessible, and search-ready from line one. I'm looking to grow with a team in Kathmandu where that rare perspective adds real, measurable value.
